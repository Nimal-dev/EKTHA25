Rules & Regulations

  • Any number of teams from each cullege is allowed. But there should be no common members between teams.
  • A team must have a minimum of 5 and a maximum of 25 members, including accompanists.
  • A minimum of 4 dancers should be present on stage at all times.
  • The time limit for each performance is 6 to 15 minutes including 10 minutes for the performance and up to 5 minutes for setup.
  • Exceeding the time limit will result in negative marking
  • Participants are allowed to choose their own music.
  • Participants must bring their college ID cards for verification
  • The music and visuals displayed on the LED screen must be submitted in MP3 or MP4 format on a pen drive at least one hour prior to the event.
  • Participants may use their own properties. However, flammable objects, weapons, or any items posing a safety risk are strictly prohibited.
  • The stage should be cleared after each performance.
  • Performances will be evaluated based on the following:
    • Choreography
    • Concept
    • Synchronization
    • Rhythm and Formations
    • Expressions and Costumes
    • Choice of Music
    • Overall Impact
  • Any form of indecent gestures, vulgarity, profanity, or obscenity will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The decision of the judges is final and binding
