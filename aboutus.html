What is
EKTHA?

Ektha 25 is the annual techno-cultural fest of UKF College of Engineering & Technology, happening on March 5, 6 and 7, 2025, exploring the balance between technology, time, and recreation.

The event features a Tech Biennale showcasing innovations from CE, CSE, and EEE branches, along with competitions like CTF, AI Prompt Challenge, Blind Coding, and IT Quiz. Attendees can experience tech exhibits, workshops on AI, DIP, CAD, and mobile repair, as well as auto expos, gaming zones, VR shows, and robotics events like Robo War and Bomb Diffuse Robo.

Cultural highlights include Nrithya'25, Melodia'25, and Aura'25, while fun activities like soapy football, paintball, and laser maze add to the excitement. Organized by student coordinators Abhishek Aravind, Anoop V Kumar, and Areena A, along with faculty convenors, the event provides a platform for learning, networking, and entertainment.

For more details, visit our Website or contact via Email ektha25@ukfcet.ac.in

Our College
UKFCET

UKF College of Engineering & Technology, established in 2009 and managed by Universal Knowledge Foundation Trust, has built a strong reputation with excellent infrastructure and dedicated faculty.

The institution emphasizes comprehensive student development through academics, soft skills training, and industry partnerships. Students excel academically and in extracurricular activities, with regular expert sessions for communication and interview skills.

The college maintains healthy relationships between staff, students, and management while constantly upgrading infrastructure.UKF College has AICTE approval for 4-year B.Tech and 2-year M.Tech degree programs in various branches.
